Advanced Play Tips 进阶玩法提示

  • 1. Numeric Fate: sum the numbers; odd picks A, even picks B.
    数字决定命运：将随机数相加，奇数选A，偶数选B
  • 2. Advanced Play: if sum > 10 pick A, otherwise pick B.
    进阶玩法：数字相加＞10选A，反之选B
  • 3. Fun Upgrade: if the product’s last digit is odd pick A, even picks B.
    趣味升级：乘积个位奇数选A，偶数选B
  • 4. Ultimate: take sum mod 3; if remainder 0 or 1 pick A, otherwise pick B.
    一决胜负：总和除以3，余数0/1选A，其他选B
